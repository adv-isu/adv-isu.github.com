Buttons in Two Panels

navigation toolbar, editing toolbar, basic

Click the colored buttons in the lower left and right corners. On a desktop browser, they behave as expected. On an iOS device, clicking the blue button immediately after a red button shows the alert, "red button clicked!". This indicates that the wrong button's trigger() method is being called. Since the buttons are in two different panels, it's probaby not a bug in the Panel code. Rather, it's probably a bug somewhere in OpenLayers/Events/buttonclick.js having to do with touch events.
